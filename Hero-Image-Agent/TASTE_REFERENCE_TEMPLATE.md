# WCM Hero Image Taste Reference
### For Trent to Complete — This Teaches the AI Your Eye

This document trains the hero-selection AI on your specific taste. The more honest and specific your answers, the better the AI will perform. You don't need to be technical — just describe what you see and feel.

Fill in Sections A and B. Aim for 10 in each. Even 5–7 will meaningfully improve the AI's selections.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS

1. Pull up a sold property from the WCM archive
2. Find the image that was selected as the hero (the lead image on the listing page or feature story)
3. Fill in one entry below — describe the image, explain why it worked or didn't
4. For "not hero" examples, pick real photos from shoots that were passed over — not trash, just not hero material
5. Save this file and share it with Mica or Claude before running a selection session
